Delaware Lease Break Penalty Calculator

Delaware law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

State cap: Delaware limits your total penalty to 2 months' rent.

Domestic violence protection: Delaware allows survivors to exit a lease without financial penalty with proper documentation.

Average re-rental time in Delaware: approximately 40 days. Required notice: 60 days.

Governing law: Del. Code Ann. tit. 25, § 5507
