New Mexico Lease Break Penalty Calculator

New Mexico law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

Domestic violence protection: New Mexico allows survivors to exit a lease without financial penalty with proper documentation.

Average re-rental time in New Mexico: approximately 45 days. Required notice: 30 days.

Governing law: N.M. Stat. Ann. § 47-8-37
