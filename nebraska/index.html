Nebraska Lease Break Laws

Landlord must mitigate 30-day notice

Nebraska law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

Average re-rental time: approximately 45 days.

Governing law: Neb. Rev. Stat. § 76-1432
