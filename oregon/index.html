Oregon Lease Break Penalty Calculator

Oregon law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

State cap: Oregon limits your total penalty to 1.5 months' rent.

Domestic violence protection: Oregon allows survivors to exit a lease without financial penalty with proper documentation.

Average re-rental time in Oregon: approximately 30 days. Required notice: 30 days.

Governing law: Or. Rev. Stat. § 90.302
