Vermont Lease Break Penalty Calculator

Vermont law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

Domestic violence protection: Vermont allows survivors to exit a lease without financial penalty with proper documentation.

Average re-rental time in Vermont: approximately 50 days. Required notice: 30 days.

Governing law: Vt. Stat. Ann. tit. 9, § 4467
