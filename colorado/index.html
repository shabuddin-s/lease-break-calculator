Colorado Lease Break Penalty Calculator

Colorado law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

Domestic violence protection: Colorado allows survivors to exit a lease without financial penalty with proper documentation.

Average re-rental time in Colorado: approximately 30 days. Required notice: 21 days.

Governing law: Colo. Rev. Stat. § 13-40-107.5
