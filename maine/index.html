Maine Lease Break Penalty Calculator

Maine law requires landlords to actively re-rent the unit — you only owe rent until a new tenant moves in.

Domestic violence protection: Maine allows survivors to exit a lease without financial penalty with proper documentation.

Average re-rental time in Maine: approximately 50 days. Required notice: 30 days.

Governing law: Me. Rev. Stat. tit. 14, § 6010-B
